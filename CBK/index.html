About Christine

A bookkeeper who's been in your shoes

When I launched my first business, I was driven by passion and purpose. But the administrative and financial side quickly pulled me away from the work I cared about most. That experience pushed me to study business and ultimately led me to a career I love: supporting business owners like you so you can thrive without the overwhelm.

If you're still reading, you already know you're stretched too thin. The average business owner saves 10 hours every month by outsourcing their bookkeeping — time you can reinvest into growth, creativity, or simply restoring balance at home.

I provide clean, accurate monthly financials tailored to your industry, giving you a clear snapshot of your cash flow, profitability, and trends — the information you need to make confident, big-picture decisions.

Let me take bookkeeping off your plate so you can reclaim your time, your clarity, and your peace of mind.

What I Offer

Services designed for growing businesses

Monthly Bookkeeping

Clean, accurate financial records every month. Income, expenses, categorized and reconciled — your books always in order and ready for decisions.

Financial Reporting

Profit & loss, balance sheets, and cash flow reports that tell a clear story — so you can make confident, growth-focused decisions.

Catch-Up Bookkeeping

Behind on your books? I'll untangle months or years of records, get you fully current, and set you up with a clean system — at 50% off.

Tax Preparation Support

Records organized and ready when your CPA needs them — reducing tax prep time and minimizing costly mistakes.

Accounts Payable & Receivable

Stay on top of what you owe and what you're owed. Invoices, payments, and vendor bills tracked so your cash flow stays healthy.

Payroll Processing

Accurate, on-time payroll for your team. I handle the numbers so your employees get paid correctly and you stay compliant.
