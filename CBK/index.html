What I Offer

Services designed for growing businesses

Monthly Bookkeeping

Clean, accurate financial records delivered every month. Income, expenses, categorized and reconciled — your books always in order and ready for decisions.

Financial Reporting

Profit & loss statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports that tell a clear story — giving you the data to make confident, growth-focused decisions.

Catch-Up Bookkeeping

Behind on your books? I'll untangle months (or years) of records, get you fully current, and set you up with a clean system going forward — at 50% off.

Tax Preparation Support

I'll make sure your records are organized and ready when your CPA needs them — reducing your tax prep time and minimizing costly mistakes.

Accounts Payable & Receivable

Stay on top of what you owe and what you're owed. I track invoices, payments, and vendor bills so your cash flow stays healthy and predictable.

Payroll Processing

Accurate, on-time payroll for your team. I handle the numbers so your employees get paid correctly and you stay compliant without the headache.
