QuickBooks Online Access
I work exclusively through QuickBooks Online. This step helps me understand where you are in the QBO process and guides you through granting me accountant access — securely, without sharing your password.

Your accounts stay secure. QuickBooks Online's accountant access feature lets me work on your books without ever needing your login credentials or access to your bank accounts directly. You maintain full control at all times.

How to Grant Accountant Access in QuickBooks Online
1
Log in to your QuickBooks Online account at quickbooks.intuit.com
2
Click the Settings ⚙️ gear icon in the top right corner
3
Under Your Company, select Manage Users
4
Click the Accounting Firms tab, then click Invite
5
Enter my firm email: Christine@claritybookkeepingbychristine.com — and send the invite
6
I'll receive a notification and confirm access — then we're ready to get started!

No QuickBooks Online account yet? No problem at all. During our consultation I'll walk you through your options and we'll find the most cost-effective path for your business. You don't need to do anything before our call.
